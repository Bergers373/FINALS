About the Page



          Ones Blog is a blogsite that is managed by the group of students of CIP1101-ITBD A.Y. 2020-2021. Led by Bernard Mazo with the help of his company including Christian Philip Orsolino, Rigoletto Giannoni, and Joshua Namoc. The unity of their group along with the coordination of each members leads to success of this website.

          What really matters to this group is the efficiency, credibility of every content they are posting in every page. Their goal is to share their thoughts by giving an informative blog that might inspire or give knowledge to the audience.

        Blogging interesting topics, arguments, opinions is the main purpose of this website. The authors want to influence their audience in a way of they are enjoying while browsing the pages. What they did is to make sure that the visual design will attract their audience and the interface is user friendly. By that, the viewers will probably browse the website for a longer period of time. The team work and hard work is present in every pages of this website.

          We promise to deliver high quality and informative content and do our best to provide to satisfy our audience in every topics we are posting.
