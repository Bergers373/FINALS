Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)





Q.

How did you get started?
A.

We started creating blog when it became our project. Eventually we became interested on creating more, we love this way of expressing our thoughts and ideas, and at the same we inspire other people.



Q.

Why this blogsite named "Ones Blog"?
A.

The first reason is because we are from group one of CIP 1101, ITBD course. Second, "Ones Blog" is telling that this blogsite is written by everyone in our group we share our thought and ideas and compiled to create an amazing content.



Q.

How hard it is to make blogs?
A.

Making blogs is not simple. The creator must be creative and unique, you should organize and manage your content to have an interesting output. All the sources you used must be valid and reliable.



Q.

How do blog sites make money?
A.

Placing ads in your websites is the most common way to make money. Company pays your website if the readers interact with ads, their are also other deals on how the company would pay you.



Q.

I want to start a blog, what is your advice?
A.

Just enjoy making it, creating blogs with your heart will lead you to success.
