Leaning Tower of Pisa



          The Leaning Tower of Pisa in Italy is one of the most remarkable architectural structures from medieval Europe that has been standing for over 800 years already without being disrupted or destroyed by earthquakes, storms, and wars. This is also one of the places I want to visit because it holds a lot of historical information that I want to learn. The leaning Tower of Pisa is the Piazza’s crowning Glory. Although it is just a third as high as the Washington Monument, it was a miracle of the medieval engineering, it is still the tallest bell tower in Europe that was ever made. The construction was done around August 1173 and continued for 200 years due to the war that is continuously happening.
