ENGLAND







The English are known for being reserved. Perhaps that's why, despite living in one of loveliest countries on Earth, they don't really shout about it. They should though. While the landscape in England lacks the drama of other parts of the world -- and even other parts of the United Kingdom -- it makes up for it with a gentle, ancient beauty that's hard to match. England! That green and pleasant land. An inspiration to poets and painters, writers and filmmakers, from Cornwall's rugged cliffs and golden beaches to the gentle rolling hills and honey-stone dwellings of the Cotswolds, and then again the granite fells and glassy meres of the Lake District. And then there are the cities.
