Glossary


Accordance

Conformity or agreement.

Adapt

Make (something) suitable for a new use or purpose; modify

Adopt

legally take (another's child) and bring it up as one's own.

Ardent

Enthusiastic or passionate.

Assurance

A positive declaration intended to give confidence; a promise.

Attained

Succeed in achieving (something that one desires and has worked for).

Avidity

Extreme eagerness or enthusiasm

Contributor

A person or thing that contributes something

Capability

The power or ability to do something.

Compiled

Produce (something, especially a list, report, or book) by assembling information collected from other sources.

Connected

Associated or related in some respect.

Deduction

The action of deducting or subtracting something.

Eagerness

Enthusiasm to do or to have something; keenness.

Ensures

Make certain that (something) shall occur or be the case
