WHAT IS THE BEST TYPE OF ONLINE LEARNING?


          Adjustments are the only thing we do yet many of us suffer from this type of learning. Many students shouldn't consider this a way of learning because we can't even understand clearly the essence of all the lessons they teach. We only take this academic year because we don't want to waste a year without attaining something. Despite all the hardships of the students, we continue to do all the tasks even if there's no learning in everything we do.

          Synchronous is the most effective type of learning for me because there is no time frame. You can do all the tasks in wherever place you wanted to do and the time you wanted to do. The feedback of the teacher to the question of students is answered instantly. In every topic, there is also a disadvantage and that's the interaction of the students and the teacher is depend on the teacher. Another disadvantage is the internet speed sometimes it can result in some difficulty or problems in the middle of the classes.

          Each of us must find the way where we are and easy for us to learn. There are many ways for us to learn just find one that will suit you as a student. In my case, Synchronous help me in this time of distance learning. This setup is slowly killing our mental health however we are going to take this as a way of strength because at the end of this academic year this will be a memorable moment that how hard the situation we deal we choose to continue until the end.
