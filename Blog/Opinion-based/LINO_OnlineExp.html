OUR ONLINE LEARNING EXPERIENCE


          First of all I was excited about learning online, I was full of hope and determined to learn. The thought of not commuting just to get there to the school is ideal for me, the thought of having class while you just laying on your bed is a dream, i thought of not getting let for class is the best. I was fully hooked by the idea of online learning, but then everything changed after 4months.

          Due to pandemic me and my friends take different paths, some stop because of money, some stop due to online learning and it’s just me trying to survive online learning. Our class starts at 7am to 4:40pm. No vacant when it’s monday. Sometimes during synchronous sessions, while the camera is off, I was eating. The temptation of cheating during online class is hard to resist and the guilt after doing it is superb. There is one time that I don't know exactly the question. Therefore, I switched tabs and googled it, and my professor gave me a warning about the violation I made. She said that if i do that again he will put a deduction on my quiz. This distance learning taught me how to handle my time properly, without time management will you have trouble handling online class. Maybe you’re the one who’s ranting on facebook saying that we’re not robot stating that professor giving them a lot of activities but here you are ranting instead of working. Our small business affected due to this pandemic luckily our parents keep this small money to sustain my education made me feel so lucky on my situation right now. Not everyone can study.

          People who experience distance learning need to adopt that this is the only key to continue and pursue our dream. We, who are currently taking online learning due to this pandemic, are lucky because not everyone can study like what we are doing right now, some need to stop even though they are scholars or something. I want everyone to do their best and study harder just like what your parents are doing just to keep your stomach and brain full. If you are having a hard time studying, just think of what our parents are doing, they are saving money just to keep us smart and safe. I know it’s hard and the passion of learning is fading but keep on fighting.
