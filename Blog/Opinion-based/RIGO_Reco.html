RECOMMENDATIONS ABOUT ONLINE LEARNING


          Covid-19 pandemic continuously affects us, we do not know when this pandemic will end. It may take a few more years for us to overcome this challenging phase of our life. What we should do is to focus on the improvement of our society’s systems including the education system/method as we might stay studying online for unknown more years.

          Current educational system somehow solves our education problems. The schools are providing different kinds of online learning methods to ensure that students won’t be stopped gaining knowledge. These solutions tend to help us, students, but somehow ade our learning more complicated and difficult.

          The following are the list of recommendations that I want to be implemented for the next academic years:

  • Subject-related activities - some instructors assigning tasks to students that are not related to the subject and there are instances where a particular student can’t do that assignment. It leads to additional stress for the student.
  • Less weekend activities - We, students, want to have a peace of mind and rest day. I’m not saying not to give or assign activities on weekends but instead, don’t assign too many tasks, difficult, complicated, or time consuming activities.
  • Consider students struggles - Struggles such as unstable internet connection, late submission due to equipment malfunction, etc.

           The world needs understanding and consideration if necessary. The effects of this pandemic hit us differently. What is possible for you may not be possible to others. Teachers are struggling too in this challenging time. We, students, should also understand, consider, and respect our teachers no matter what.
