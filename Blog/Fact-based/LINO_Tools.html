What Is Google Classroom?

          Google Classroom enables teachers to create an online classroom area in which they can manage all the documents that their students need. Documents are stored on Google Drive and can be edited in Drive’s apps, such as Google Docs, Sheets, and so on. But what separates Google Classroom from the regular Google Drive experience is the teacher/student interface, which Google designed for the way teachers and students think and work

What Is Google Docs?

          Google Docs is a free Web-based application in which documents and spreadsheets can be created, edited and stored online. Files can be accessed from any computer with an Internet connection and a full-featured Web browser. Google Docs is a part of a comprehensive package of online applications offered by and associated with Google. Talk instead of type For times when you'd rather use your voice than your fingertips to type, give Google Docs' voice typing tool a try.

What Is Google Form?

          Google Forms can collect people's information via custom quizzes, registrations or surveys. The information can then be connected to a table on sheets to record the answer automatically. The table then supplies the answers in real time from the test or survey. Google Forms is therefore one of the easiest ways of saving data directly to a table.

What Is Google Keep?

          You can create and add images to notes, check tasks off your to-do list. With Google Keep, you can create, share, and collaborate with people on notes and lists as well. Keep synchronizes across all your devices, so your notes and lists can go with you wherever you are since it is a cloud based application. Google Keep is only available offline when using a Chromebook AND the Google Keep Android app from the Google Play Store. Google Keep Offline is not available on Windows and macOS devices os, or simply a personal computer without an internet.

What Is Google Meet?

          Google Meet makes it easy to start a secure video meeting. Join from any modern web browser or download the app, and you're ready to go. Anyone with a Google Account can create an online meeting with up to 100 participants and meet for up to 60 minutes per meeting. Businesses, schools, and other organizations can take advantage of advanced features, including meetings with up to 250 internal or external participants and live streaming to up to 100,000 viewers within a domain.

What Is Google Calendar?

          Google calendar lets you schedule meetings and events quickly and get reminders about upcoming activities, so always know what’s next. Calendar is designed for teams, so it’s easy to share your schedule with others and create multiple calendars that you and your team can utilize together.

What Is Google Scholar?

          The switch to online education has disrupted everyone's schedule. Time management is one of the main challenges that learners have to face. Provides a simple way to broadly search for scholarly literature. From one place, you can search across many disciplines and sources like articles, theses, books, abstracts and court opinions, from academic publishers, professional societies, online repositories, universities and other web sites. Google scholar can be used by students and researchers to collect some data fortheir study/work
