Online Learning: Advantages and Disadvantages, Types of Online Learning


          The Covid-19 pandemic has triggered many modes of learning. One of these is distance/online learning. Online learning became the new normal in education, most of the countries around the world use this especially the country with many covid-19 positive cases rate.

          Online learning, also known as “e-learning”, is a type of education that takes place over the internet. It is the most popular and widely used mode of learning due to the global crisis we are experiencing right now. It really changed the way of teaching and studying. Unlike the traditional way of learning that students and teachers are interacting with each other face to face, the online type of learning gives a lot of options on how the students study in distance. These options give students and teachers convenience but there are a lot of factors that affect the efficiency of education.

Advantages and Disadvantages

          Online learning is a great alternative way of learning especially for those people who can’t attend face-to-face classes such as persons with disabilities and people who can’t afford time and money to take the real courses. What are the advantages and disadvantages of online learning?

Advantages

          Online learning is very convenient, it takes less effort to attend meetings and do tasks. Some modes require no synchronous meetings and you can find your free time to do assignments and submit in specified time. Internet connection is the only key to connect with you instructors and to download needed materials. You can also choose what time to study the provided lectures.

Disadvantages

          Social connections are limited, it affects how students interact. Human interaction plays a big role in character development of students. Another disadvantage is low efficiency learning. Unlike in traditional education that the teachers keep in touch with students, teachers in online class can’t maintain the discipline and student’s eagerness to learn since he/she doesn't know/see if the students are still listening.

Conclusion

          In conclusion, traditional learning shouldn’t be replaced by online learning because it has a lot of features that provide effective learning. Online learning is changing the capability of the students to learn, we must hope that this pandemic will end very soon so that we can come back to the real normal.
