Online Learning: Advantages and Disadvantages, Types of Online Learning


          The different types of online learning in the Philippines have various features that provide best and quality education for the students. Here are the different types of online learning.

Synchronous - this type includes virtual meetings like you are in a classroom, it includes video conferencing and chat sessions. Students are required to attend meetings and be marked as absent if he/she didnâ€™t attend the online class meeting(video or chat session). It is the most common type of online learning since it made the professor and students interact with each other easily. It is mostly used on online examination so that the teachers can monitor their students while taking the exam.
Asynchronous - It is a type of online learning that is very flexible. It gives students freedom to study on their own. Sometimes it is difficult, as the professor's instructions are only given through online learning platform chats, email, and other text media.
Hybrid - It is the combination of synchronous and asynchronous. Students are required to attend fixed time for conference meetings or chat rooms, and can also submit their assignments or asynchronous activities online. It usually has a schedule where there is a target hours or days for both synchronous and asynchronous.
